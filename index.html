IUB Organizing Hub

A clearinghouse for media, petitions, and other events related to organizing against injustice at Indiana University Bloomington.

Cancelling Samia Halaby (Jan. 2024) [Links]

Palestinian artist, activist, and educator Samia Halaby's first retrospective exhibition in the U.S. is cancelled by Indiana University, citing "safety and security," as well as Halaby's Instagram posts advocating for Palestinian liberation.

Suspending Abdulkader Sinno (Jan. 2024) [Links]

In direct response to Sinno's role as advisor of IUB's Palestinian Solidarity Committee, the university suspended him for the Spring/Summer 2024 semesters for holding an event to support Palestinians.
