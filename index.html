Grievances


Cancelling Samia Halaby (Jan. 2024) [Links]

Palestinian artist, activist, and educator Samia Halaby's first retrospective exhibition in the U.S. is cancelled by Indiana University, citing "safety and security," as well as Halaby's Instagram posts advocating for Palestinian liberation.

Suspending Abdulkader Sinno (Jan. 2024) [Links]

In direct response to Sinno's role as advisor of IUB's Palestinian Solidarity Committee, the university suspended him for the Spring/Summer 2024 semesters for holding an event to support Palestinians.

Political Incursions on Academic Freedom [Links]

Indiana Senate Bill 202 would undermine tenure and put IU under stricter political control. This follows the November 2023 letter to IUB from Jim Banks, pressuring them to curb academic freedoms in response to protests for Palestinian liberation. IUB's administration seems to acquiesce.

Shuttering the Kinsey Institute [Links]

In April 2023, the Indiana General Assembly voted to ban providing funds to the historic and world famous Kinsey Institute, one of the originators of the study of sexuality.

Pamela Whitten Undermines Faculty Governance at IUPUI (2023) [Links]

Using the breakup of IUPUI as justification, Pamela Whitten and IU's administration undermine tenure at the institution, putting at risk dozens of professors.
