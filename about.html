IUB Organizing Hub

A clearinghouse for media, petitions, and other events related to organizing against injustice at Indiana University Bloomington.

This site acts as a hub for news, media, petitions, and events around injustice on the campus of Indiana University Bloomington (IUB). This site is collectively managed by the Indiana University Bloomington Organizing Collective (IUB-OC), a loosely affiliated group composed of faculty, graduate students, undergraduates, and alumni from IUB.

To contribute or get involved with this site, please email iub.org.collective[at]gmail.com. Feel free to send anything you may think is relevant. If there are things going on that are not included here, please include a one sentence summary.
